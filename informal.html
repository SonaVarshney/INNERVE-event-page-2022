Toon Guesser

Overview

Innerve organized an exciting, nostalgic, and fun-filled event, Toon Guesser, where participants had to guess the cartoon from the audio and video clippings posted on social media stories.

Two Truth and a Loki Lie

Overview

Two Truths and a Loki Lie, a thrilling and engrossing game where players had to identify a lie from options provided on social media stories, was organized by Innerve to test all the Marvel fans.

Guess the Correct Logo

Overview

Innerve organised, Guess the Logo, a thrilling and entertaining game, that required participants to identify the logo from an image posted on social media stories in order to illustrate their capacity for observation.
